Competition Rules!

Duration:

The competition will run for six weeks.

Initial funds:

RM50,000/account.

Tradable assets:

Participants can trade stocks listed on Bursa Malaysia. You can view the list of stocks HERE.

Trading hours:

Participants can place trades anytime during the trading week (Monday to Friday), during regular market hours (9:00 AM - 12:30 PM and 2:30PM - 5:00 PM MST).

Trading restrictions:

i.   Participants are not allowed to trade on margin or short sell a stock.

ii.  Participants' account balance must be less than 50% by 5:00 PM daily to encourage active trading. If their balance is greater than or equal       to 50%, they will be disqualified from the competition.

Scoring system:

Participants will earn points based on their profit and loss (P&L) during the competition period. The formula for calculating points is as follows:

Points = (P&L / Starting Account Balance) x 100.

Leaderboard:

The app will display a leaderboard that shows the top 10 participants ranked by their total points earned during the competition period.

Notifications:

Participants will receive notifications when they enter or exit a position, and when their P&L crosses certain thresholds (e.g., RM1000 profit or RM500 loss).

Analytics and reporting:

The app provides participants with access to a dashboard where they can view their P&L, account balance, open positions, and trade history. Participants can also generate reports that show their performance metrics during the competition period.
